Companies We Love

Cobra Tactical Firearms

Our personal choice for any gun shop needs. Kim and Eric at Cobra Tactical have done nothing but provide amazing service and products to us and are extremely knowledgable and professional

Liberty Safe

While we don't own one of these amazing safes ourselves, every customer of Liberty Safes we've spoken to wouldn't choose anything else. Liberty Safes is known for great customer service and great products.

T-Rex Arms

T-Rex Arm's Lucas Botkin is an amazing proponent of the Second Amendment and makes great innovative products. Their holsters aren't only used by civilians but also by various government agencies both in the U.S. and internationally.

Project Appleseed

The knowledge gained by attending a Project Appleseed event is absolutely unmatched. Whether you've never shot a firearm or have been shooting all your life, there's never anyone who leaves a Project Appleseed event without improving their skills - and knowledge of the history behind things like the Revolutionary War.

Stag Arms Rifles and Accessories

Any product from Stag Arms is likely a good product. Especially recommended due to their option to have rifles and parts shipped to be compliant to various states laws and regulations. Amazing for those in states like California, New York, and New Jersey.
